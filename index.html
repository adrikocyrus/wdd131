📍 Kampala, Uganda

Kampala is the capital and largest city of Uganda. It is located near the northern shores of Lake Victoria and serves as the country's main center for government, business, and culture. The city is known for its mix of modern buildings, busy markets, and historical sites. Kampala has a lively atmosphere with strong cultural traditions, a growing urban population, and serves as an important hub for transportation and commerce in the region.
